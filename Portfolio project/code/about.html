REBECCA SPIN

SOPHOMORE | UC DAVIS | GRAPHIC DESIGN MAJOR

Rebecca Spin is currently an undergraduate design major at UC Davis. Her study interests pinpoint graphic design and psychology. She does freelance design on commission, and has worked with patrons such as the Youth Assistance Coalition, Kehillah Jewish High School, and Student Affairs Marketing and Communications at UC Davis. She has created logos, decals, t-shirt designs, snapchat filters, flyers for events, and more. In the future, she hopes to branch out into animation and storyboarding.
